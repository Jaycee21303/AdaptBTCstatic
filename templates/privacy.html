Privacy

We protect your data the way we protect our Bitcoin operations.

Clear commitments on what we collect, how we use it, and the controls you keep.

What we collect

  • Form submissions: names, emails, and context you share on consulting or learning requests.
  • Operational telemetry: anonymized logs to keep the site responsive and secure.
  • No tracking bloat: we do not sell data or run ad pixels.

How we use it

  • Respond to consulting and learning inquiries with relevant guidance.
  • Secure platform uptime, including rate limiting and abuse prevention.
  • Improve tools like the DCA Lab and Signal Engine with aggregate insights.

Where it lives

  • Encrypted transit by default for every request.
  • Limited retention with periodic reviews for deletion.
  • Access controls restricted to AdaptBTC operators who need it.

Your controls

We keep the knobs obvious so you can move quickly without sacrificing privacy.

Request deletion

If you have submitted consulting details, email support@adaptbtc.com to remove them. We confirm within two business days.

Data access

Ask what data we hold about you and we will share a human-readable summary with timestamps and sources.

Cookie stance

We only use cookies necessary to keep sessions secure; analytics are aggregate and privacy-friendly.

Security practices

Built with the same operational rigor as our Bitcoin custody recommendations.

Transport

HTTPS everywhere, certificate monitoring, and rate limits tuned to protect tools from abuse.

Storage

Secrets live in environment variables, never in the repo. Access is logged and reviewed regularly.

Review cadence

Privacy page and runbooks carry a last reviewed date so you know when we last audited our stance.
