Educational Sidebar

What is the mempool?

It’s the waiting room for unconfirmed Bitcoin transactions. Miners select from here when building the next block.

How confirmations work

Each new block adds one confirmation. Exchanges and merchants often require multiple confirmations for safety.

On-chain vs Lightning

On-chain settles directly to the blockchain with higher finality, while Lightning offers instant, low-fee payments off-chain.
